More than Fiber

Since 1969, Universal Fibers® has led the industry in the development and
production of high performance, sustainable, solution-dyed carpet fiber.
Our mission is to establish inclusive partnered relationships and work in
collaboration to deliver leading innovation custom tailored to meet the
unique and varied needs of our customers. Universal Fibers is not a one
size fits all company.

Sustainable Products. For over 50 years, Universal Fibers® has
